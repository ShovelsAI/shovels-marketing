Recently, I teamed up with <a href="https://www.credaily.com/briefs/nationwide-cre-new-construction-permits-fell-16-in-q1-2026/" target="_blank">CRE Daily to dig into Q1 2026 commercial construction permit data</a>. The trends tell an interesting story.

We saw that nationwide, new CRE permits fell 16% year-over-year, with multifamily taking the biggest hit at -29%.

But it wasn't all bad news: office permits actually ticked up 4%, New York rebounded strongly, and North Carolina held its ground while Florida and Texas cooled off significantly.
